# Pizza Sales Data Analysis (Interactive Dashboard using MS Excel)

## Project Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze pizza sales data to gain insights into overall business performance, customer ordering patterns, and product popularity. This analysis helps identify key trends in revenue, order behavior, and pizza preferences, which can support data-driven decision-making to improve sales strategy and operational efficiency.

## Dataset Used

* <a href="https://github.com/indradityaa/Pizza-Sales-Analytics-Dashboard/blob/main/pizza_sales.csv">Dataset</a>

## Questions (KPIs)

* What is the **total revenue** generated?
* What is the **average order value**?
* What is the **total number of pizzas sold**?
* What is the **total number of orders**?
* What is the **average number of pizzas per order**?
* What is the **daily trend for total orders**?
* What is the **hourly trend for total orders**?
* What is the **percentage of sales by pizza category**?
* What is the **percentage of sales by pizza size**?
* How many pizzas are sold by each **pizza category**?
* What are the **top 5 best-selling pizzas** by total pizzas sold?
* What are the **bottom 5 worst-selling pizzas** by total pizzas sold?

## Process

* Performed data cleaning by checking for missing values, duplicates, and inconsistencies.
* Standardized data formats for date, time, and numerical fields.
* Created calculated fields to derive KPIs such as total revenue and average order value.
* Built pivot tables to answer each KPI question.
* Designed an interactive Excel dashboard by combining all pivot tables.
* Added slicers and filters to enable dynamic analysis.

## Project Insights

* Sales performance can be clearly tracked through revenue and order-based KPIs.
* Certain pizza categories and sizes contribute a higher percentage of total sales.
* Customer ordering behavior shows clear daily and hourly trends.
* A small group of pizzas dominates total sales, while some items consistently underperform.

## Final Conclusion

This pizza sales analysis provides valuable insights into customer preferences and sales trends. By focusing on top-performing pizza categories and best-selling products, the business can optimize its menu and inventory planning. Additionally, understanding peak order times and low-performing items enables better marketing strategies, promotional planning, and operational improvements to maximize overall revenue.
